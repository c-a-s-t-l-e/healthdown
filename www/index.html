About Healthdown

Healthdown App

The Healthdown app provides an interactive comparison of various health factors at the U.S. state and county level.
  • Compare various health factors across states or counties.
  • Quickly find regions of interest with the search function.
  • Switch between various factors displayed on the map.

Data Source

The annual rankings by the University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute provide an insightful snapshot of how health is affected by various factors.

The leafdown package

The leafdown package adds drilldown functionality for leaflet choropleths.
  • Select regions of interest and visualize corresponding data in other dashboard elements.
  • Use the flexible drilldown functionality for detailed exploration.
  • Efficiently change the displayed variables without redrawing the map.
  • Change map backgrounds on runtime.
